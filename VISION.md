# Vision

University regulations are not plain text.

They are structured legal documents.

UniReg does not build a chatbot.

UniReg builds structured knowledge from regulations.

LLMs consume structured knowledge.

The parser is the foundation.

Every answer must be explainable.

Every answer must cite its legal source.

The parser should support:

- University regulations
- Research regulations
- HR regulations
- Financial regulations
- Government regulations

The long-term goal is to become the standard parser for Korean institutional regulations.
